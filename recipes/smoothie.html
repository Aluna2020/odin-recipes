Immune system boosting smoothie

As we all know health is very important for longevity. Eating healthy does not need to always be plain and boring, there are many ways to make dieting fun while reaping the benefits. One espactacular way to getting all the necesary nutrients is by making smoothies, it is very simple and easy to make. I will guide you into making tasty smoothies while building a healthier you. Are you ready !? let's get started.

ingredients

  • a carrot
  • a banana
  • clementine
  • ginger
  • orange juice>
  • lemon juice
  • tumeric
  • cinnamon
  • yogurt (preferably fat free greek yogurt)
  • ice

(If you have a sweet tooth, you may add sweetner)

steps to making the smoothie

  1. Place all ingredients into your belnder
  2. Blend until all items are completly mixed together
  3. Test to see if smoothie is sweet enough if not (you may add a bit more sweetner)
  4. Pour it up pour into your favorite cup and enjoy!

(For sweetners i recommend to go with natural)
