Protein pizza

Do you consider yourself a pizza lover like me?! of course you are who doesn't love pizza right, that's why you are here. We all know that constantly eating pizza day in and day out is generally not healthy for you, but what if i was to tell you theres an healthier and alternative way to have your pizza. this is a easy preparation with simple ingredients as well, so take out your notepad and take notes as you read along this blog to create your own tasty pizza. I promise it is not a long preperation and cooking time, the plan here is to enjoy the goodness with little time to waste!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup self rising flour
  • 1/4 cup fat free greek yogurt plain
  • 1/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/2 chicken sausasge cooked
  • 1/4 cup fat-free cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoon fat free feta cheese(optional)
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
  • veggies of choice (spinach, brocoli, mushrooms)

Steps

  1. pre-heat oven to about 475 degrees
  2. mix flour, yeast, along with the greek yogurt until it becomes a dough ball
  3. now put the dough ball into an oil sprayed parchment paper, place another paper on top and use a rolling pin until it's flattened out
  4. now you may add your toppings onto your pizza, then place pizza onto a pre-heated sheet pan do not remove parchment paper)
  5. last but not least place the pan into the oven, and lower the oven to 450 degrees, and bake for about 8 minutes. Voila ! your pizza is ready to eat.
