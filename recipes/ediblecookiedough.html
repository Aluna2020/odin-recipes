Edible Cookie Dough

in this article i will show you how to make the number 1 best edible cookie dough on the planet! You can share the goodness with your friends and family, or even decide to include this in your special occassion treats.

ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (this eliminates any bacteria)
  • Granulated sugar and Brown sugar (this provides the recipe with the classic flavor)
  • Unsalted butter/salted butter (which ever one you prefer)
  • salt
  • Vanilla extract (use real vanilla for top notch flavor goodness)
  • Milk/almound milk
  • chocolate chips

steps to make cookie dough

  1. Heat treat the flour (place 3 cups of flour on a rimmed 18 by 13 inch baking sheet. Bake for 7 minutes until the flour reaches a temperature of 160 degrees. Allow to cool off then proceed to place flour into container
  2. Cream butters, sugars, and salt (add these ingredients in a mixing bowl and either by hand or a electric hand mixer begin to mix for 3 minutes.)
  3. add in the liquids (add milk and vanilla extract and mix)
  4. Blend in the flour (mix the flour with the ingredients)
  5. Now throw in the chocolate chips! (and mix it up)

And there you have it the edible cookie dough is complete, you can keep the cookie dough in your fridge for one week. Or you can even freeze it and it will last two months.

Tip !

You can even add more flavor by adding the cookie dough to any ice cream of your choice !
